CH340 CH341 serial adapters on macOS 10.12 Sierra

It took me some hours to make my macOS 10.12 Sierra installation to not panic after plugging in arduino clones with CH340 USB2Serial chips. This tzapu.com blog post helped me. They also provide a download link to a signed kernel extension for CH340 USB2Serial which I installed on my system.

Good luck!
